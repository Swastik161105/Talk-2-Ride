Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 21, 2025

Talk 2 Ride is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our service.

1. Information We Collect

  • Location data (for pickup and destination)
  • Voice commands (converted to text locally on your device)
  • Basic booking details (for showing past rides)
  • Authentication details if you log in via third-party (like Uber)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To show nearby cab options
  • To estimate fares and arrival times
  • To help you book a cab using voice input
  • To improve your experience using the app

3. Data Sharing

We do not share your personal data with any third party unless required to process your cab booking through a cab provider (e.g., Uber).

4. Cookies & Tracking

This website does not use cookies or trackers. All data is processed locally or via secure APIs.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your information. Voice data is not stored. Location and booking data are handled securely.

6. Your Consent

By using Talk 2 Ride, you consent to this Privacy Policy.
